## Voiceover Tips

### Pacing:
- **Normal speaking pace:** ~150 words per minute
- **For 30 seconds:** Aim for 75-90 words
- **Pause points:** After each paragraph break
- **Emphasis words:** "ATS-optimized", "CrewAI", "Version 2", "open-source SLM"

### Tone:
- **Professional but approachable**
- **Confident, not salesy**
- **Clear and articulate**
- **Slight excitement on benefits**

### Delivery Notes:
1. **Opening (0-5s):** Hook with the problem - speak clearly, slightly slower
2. **Solution (5-15s):** Explain the solution - pick up pace, show confidence
3. **Benefits (15-22s):** Highlight results - emphasize key words
4. **V2 Mention (22-26s):** Future-proofing - professional, forward-looking
5. **CTA (26-30s):** Call to action - strong, clear, inviting
